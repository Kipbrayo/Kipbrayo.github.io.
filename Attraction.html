Top Attractions in Nairobi

Nairobi National Park

Nairobi National Park is a unique wildlife reserve located just outside the bustling city center of Nairobi. It is home to a variety of wildlife including lions, giraffes, and zebras, against the backdrop of Nairobi's skyline.

Giraffe Centre

The Giraffe Centre is a conservation and education center that provides visitors with the opportunity to get up close and personal with endangered Rothschild giraffes. Visitors can feed and learn about these majestic creatures.

Karen Blixen Museum

The Karen Blixen Museum, located in the former home of the famous Danish author Karen Blixen, offers insights into colonial life in Kenya. It is surrounded by beautiful gardens and provides a glimpse into the life of Karen Blixen, author of "Out of Africa".
